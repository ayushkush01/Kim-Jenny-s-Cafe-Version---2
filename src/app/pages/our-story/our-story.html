How It Started

Two Friends.
One Dream.

Kim and Jenny didn't set out to open a cafe. They set out to create a place that felt like home — for themselves and for everyone in Duncanville who needed one.

Armed with a vision, a whole lot of determination, and hands that weren't afraid to get dirty, they built something real. Something that belongs to this neighborhood.

Today, Kim & Jenny's is more than a cafe. It's where Duncanville comes to start the day, catch up with neighbors, and feel at home. And yes — if you come often enough, your name goes on a mug.
