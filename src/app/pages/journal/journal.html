About Us

Our Story

We built Kim & Jenny's as the cafe we always wished we had nearby: real food, excellent coffee, and a room that feels familiar from your first visit.

Family-Owned Since 2014

We opened with two borrowed espresso grinders and a counter made from reclaimed wood. In those first months, neighbors brought in bookshelves, local artists hung prints on our walls, and weekend musicians played for tips and coffee.

Our breakfast menu still includes our grandmother's biscuit recipe. Our lunch plates are inspired by the meals we grew up eating in Texas kitchens - simple, comforting, and generous.

Every season we tweak the menu based on what regulars ask for, and we keep the classics because they have become part of people's routines.

Community Table

Our long table by the window stays open for students, remote workers, and neighbors catching up.

Small Batch Coffee

We roast in small runs for steady quality and adjust grinds daily based on humidity and temperature.

Neighborhood First

From school fundraisers to weekend events, we keep this cafe rooted in Duncanville.
